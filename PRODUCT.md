# DeskOps Product Context

## Register

product

## Users

DeskOps is for individuals balancing personal administration across health, family, money, home, relationships, and work. Its Build Week demo centres on an office-based knowledge worker or freelancer who already understands task systems but needs one place for the work of real life.

## Product Purpose

DeskOps turns unstructured life admin into an intelligent, reviewable queue. It makes imbalance visible through a live Wheel of Life and keeps people in control by letting AI agents draft, rather than silently execute, every meaningful action.

## Brand Personality

Calm, capable, and candid. It should feel operationally clear without treating the user like an employee or reducing their life to a corporate dashboard.

## Anti-references

Avoid productivity-guilt mechanics, gamified wellbeing apps, noisy enterprise dashboards, and generic AI chat surfaces. Do not imply medical, financial, or therapeutic advice.

## Design Principles

1. One clear next action beats an overwhelming list.
2. Make life balance visible, not abstract.
3. AI proposes; the person decides.
4. Bring operational clarity to personal life without corporate theatre.
5. Keep sensitive life context private, legible, and easy to correct.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. The Wheel must never be colour-only: provide text labels and numeric values. Respect reduced-motion preferences and use UK English throughout.
